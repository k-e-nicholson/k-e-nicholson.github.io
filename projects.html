PRT Calculator

A calculator for generating the Physical Readiness Test (PRT) scores for the United States Naval Sea Cadets Corps. This project was completed in Java, with the GUI done with JFrame.
By completing this project, the USNSCC no longer needed to calculate their scores by hand using a chart. It also allowed for the cadets and their parents to calculate the scores themselves when training at home.
This calculator reduced time spent by 83%.
